## Plan: Building Constructor redesign content transfer

### Goal
Transfer the business information, service content, trust signals, coverage details, contact details, and supporting resource topics from the live North London extensions page into the current premium redesign without changing the established visual language, colours, fonts, or animation direction.

### Constraints
- Keep the existing premium expensive aesthetic.
- Preserve the redesigned layout language and motion style.
- Make the page fully responsive across phones, desktops, and larger monitors.
- Improve accessibility where possible during the content transfer.
- Reuse live-site business details accurately while rewriting for a cleaner premium presentation.

### Steps
1. Extract live-site headings, services, office/contact details, areas served, trust/value messaging, and supporting resource topics.
2. Audit the current Next.js landing page sections and identify where each content group should live.
3. Create a shared content source so business information stays consistent across components.
4. Update hero, stats, showcase, process, and contact sections with live-site business content.
5. Add new premium sections for services, area coverage, and supporting resources.
6. Improve navigation for responsiveness and accessibility, especially on mobile.
7. Validate styling consistency, responsiveness, and build health.

### Content mapping
- Hero: business positioning, North London specialism, primary contact points.
- Expertise/Stats: years of insight, coverage footprint, service breadth.
- Services: full construction and extension service list.
- Extension Types: single-storey, double-storey, rear & loft, wrap-around, lean-to, side return, kitchen.
- Process: consultation, planning/compliance, build, handover.
- Areas: London regions, featured areas, borough coverage, office details.
- Contact: phone, email, office, enquiry form.
- Footer/Resources: blog topics, regulation references, social channels.

### Refinement notes
- Keep copy elevated and concise rather than pasting source text verbatim.
- Use clear section anchors for navigation.
- Ensure CTAs remain visible on smaller screens.
- Keep the final page premium, calm, and conversion-focused.
